About Nexora

India's Trusted Solar Infrastructure Partner

Nexora Power Projects is a leading solar EPC company delivering complete plant construction services for large developers, public institutions, commercial groups, and industrial energy users.

Established in 2015 and incorporated in 2021, the company combines Class-1 electrical credentials with deep execution discipline across ground mount, floating, rooftop, tracker, O&M, and panel manufacturing workstreams.
